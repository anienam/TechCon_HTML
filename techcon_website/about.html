Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts and innovators. Over the years, it has evolved into a premier international conference attracting thousands of participants from around the globe. From showcasing early AI research to today's groundbreaking advancements in cloud computing and blockchain, TechCon has consistently been at the forefront of technological evolution.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to inspire and empower the global technology community. By providing a platform for collaboration, education, and innovation, TechCon seeks to foster ideas that solve real-world problems and shape the future of humanity. We believe in diversity, inclusivity, and the transformative power of technology for good.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Jane Smith

Dr. Jane Smith, a leading AI researcher at MIT, shared insights on ethical artificial intelligence and has been instrumental in developing frameworks for responsible AI development.

Michael Johnson

Michael Johnson, an early pioneer in blockchain technology, spoke about the potential of decentralized finance to reshape global economies and promote financial inclusion.

Prof. Aisha Bello

Prof. Aisha Bello, a world-renowned cybersecurity expert, highlighted the importance of data protection and shared strategies for safeguarding critical infrastructure.
